ANIMAL WORD GAME

The rules of this game are simple. (1) I will give you an animal, (2) then you will follow up with an animal that starts with the last letter of that word. (3) After that, I will give you an animal that starts with the last letter of your animal. (4) This cycle will repeat until a person someone loses. If you use an animal that was alread used, you lose. If you do not give an animal, you lose. I know my animals very well, so do not try anything.

Click "PLAY" when ready to play
