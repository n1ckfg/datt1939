FOUR FIVE NO SIX

The rules of this game are very simple. We each take a turn rolling two dice, but I take the first roll. If you roll a 4 and 5, you automatically win. If you roll a double and the other person does not, you win. If you roll a bigger double than me, you win. If we both roll a non-double and you have the biggest number, you wins. VICE VERSA!
